(*Eng*) My flowers  
>My mom had given her best flowers to me  
I took, I looked.  
They were faded,  
I couldn't look..  

---
(*Tr*) Çiçeklerim  
>Annem en güzel çiçeklerini bana vermişti.  
Aldım, baktım.  
Kurudular,  
Bakamadım..
